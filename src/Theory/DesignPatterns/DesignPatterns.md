# Design Patterns

## What are design patterns?

Design patterns are proven answers to recurring design problems. They are not finished code; they are reusable abstractions and best practices.

![What design patterns are; GoF types: Creational (5), Structural (7), Behavioral (11)](assets/01-overview-design-patterns.png)

## Creational patterns

They deal with **object creation** mechanisms.

![Creational patterns: Singleton, Factory, Abstract Factory, Builder, Prototype](assets/02-creational-patterns.png)

## Structural patterns

They focus on how **classes and objects are composed** into larger structures and on making components work together despite **incompatible interfaces**.

![Structural patterns: Adapter, Bridge, Composite, Decorator, Facade, Flyweight, Proxy](assets/03-structural-patterns.png)

## Behavioral patterns

They are concerned with **communication between objects**.

![Behavioral patterns: Chain of responsibility through Visitor (11 patterns)](assets/04-behavioral-patterns.png)
